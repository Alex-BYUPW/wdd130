White Water Rafting Co.

At White Water Rafting Co., our mission is to deliver unforgettable river adventures while keeping safety and stewardship of the water at the heart of everything we do. Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned paddler, we craft every trip to leave you breathless, soaked, and smiling.

History

Founded by a small group of river guides, White Water Rafting Co. began with a single raft and a love for the water. Over the years we've grown into a full-service outfitter, guiding thousands of guests down some of the most thrilling rapids in the region, all while staying true to our roots of small-group, safety-first adventure.
