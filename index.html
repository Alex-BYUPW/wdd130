Indiana

Indiana is a state in the Midwestern United States. It is bordered by Lake Michigan to the northwest, Michigan to the north, Ohio to the east, Kentucky to the south, and Illinois to the west. The state capital and largest city, Indianapolis, is home to the Indianapolis Motor Speedway, host of the famous Indy 500 race, and is known for its friendly Midwestern culture.
